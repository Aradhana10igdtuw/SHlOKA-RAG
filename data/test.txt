Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) is a prominent herb in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
It is classified as an adaptogen, helping the body manage stress and anxiety.
The root of the Ashwagandha plant is commonly ground into powder and consumed.
Historically, it is believed to promote youthfulness, vigor, and overall longevity.
Modern research suggests it may also support brain function and quality sleep.
